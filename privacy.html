Privacy Policy for Habits

Effective Date: April 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Accion Diaria (at26) ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with your use of the Habits Android application (the "App").

1. Information We Do Not Collect Automatically

We respect your privacy. The App is designed to function without automatically collecting any personally identifiable information (PII) or sensitive data from you or your device. We do not automatically track your usage, location, device identifiers, or other personal data.

2. Information You Voluntarily Provide (Feedback Form)

The only way we collect personal information through the App is when you voluntarily choose to provide it to us via the built-in feedback form. This form may ask for information such as:

  • Your name (optional)
  • Your email address (optional, but required if you wish to receive a response)
  • Your feedback, suggestions, or bug reports

Purpose of Collecting Feedback: The information you submit through the feedback form is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To understand user feedback and improve the App's features and performance.
  • To identify and fix potential bugs or issues within the App.
  • To respond to your inquiries or comments, if you provide contact information and indicate you desire a response.

Providing this information is entirely optional. If you choose not to use the feedback form, the core functionality of the App will not be affected.

3. How We Use Your Information

Any information you voluntarily provide through the feedback form will be used exclusively for the purposes stated in Section 2. We will not use this information for marketing purposes, nor will we sell, trade, or rent it to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share the information you provide via the feedback form with any third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • If required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process.
  • When we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect the information you submit via the feedback form from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, please be aware that no electronic transmission or storage system is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised Privacy Policy within the App or on our website. Your continued use of the App after any changes or revisions to this Privacy Policy shall indicate your agreement with the terms of such revised Privacy Policy. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
